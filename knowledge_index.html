Fertilizers & Pesticides

Explore government-approved products with their uses and prices to avoid being misled by shops.

Urea

Type: Fertilizer

Use: Nitrogen source for rice, wheat, sugarcane

Price (MRP): ₹266 per 45 kg bag

Approval: Regulated under Nutrient-Based Subsidy (NBS) and Fertilizer Control Order (FCO)

Di-Ammonium Phosphate (DAP)

Type: Fertilizer

Use: Phosphorus and nitrogen for oilseeds, pulses, cereals

Price (MRP): ₹1,350 per 50 kg bag

Approval: Regulated under NBS, ₹3,500/MT subsidy in 2025

Muriate of Potash (MOP)

Type: Fertilizer

Use: Potassium for fruits, vegetables, plantation crops

Price (MRP): ₹1,600 per 50 kg bag

Approval: Regulated under NBS

NPK (10:26:26)

Type: Fertilizer

Use: Balanced nutrients for horticulture, cash crops

Price (MRP): ₹1,300–₹1,600 per 50 kg bag

Approval: Regulated under NBS

Nano DAP

Type: Fertilizer

Use: Efficient phosphorus for cereals, pulses

Price (MRP): ₹600 per 500 ml bottle

Approval: Approved by Ministry of Agriculture, promoted for 2025

Imidacloprid 17.8% SL

Type: Pesticide

Use: Controls sucking pests (aphids, whiteflies) in cotton, rice

Price (MRP): ₹500–₹700 per liter

Approval: Approved under Central Insecticides Board (CIB)

Mancozeb 75% WP

Type: Pesticide

Use: Fungicide for fungal diseases in vegetables, fruits

Price (MRP): ₹300–₹400 per kg

Approval: Approved under CIB

Chlorpyrifos 20% EC

Type: Pesticide

Use: Controls termites, borers in cereals, pulses

Price (MRP): ₹400–₹600 per liter

Approval: Approved under CIB, restricted use

Prices are approximate MRPs as of 2025. Verify with local dealers and check for subsidies to ensure fair pricing.
